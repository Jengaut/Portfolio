About me ?

I am Jennifer Gauthier, a 28-year-old graphic designer in career transition. After several years of experience in prepress, I decided to pursue training in web development due to my interest in website reproduction during my vocational training in graphic design. Today, I am happy to apply my graphic design skills to the creation of innovative websites, and I am constantly seeking new challenges to improve my abilities.

As a web developer, I have gained experience in programming using Visual Studio Code and primarily working with languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. My experience in web development has also extended to WordPress, which I used during my vocational training in graphic design, as well as creating a plug-in as part of a team project during my AEC. The plug-in design involved the use of project management tools such as Trello, which contributed to effective collaboration among team members and the project's success.
